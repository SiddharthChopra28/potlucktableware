About Us

Potluck Tableware is an extension of one of India's most versatile and experienced printing and packaging companies, Vidya Printing Company, backed by over 40 years of industry expertise.

Our advanced manufacturing infrastructure ensures consistent quality while supporting high-volume production needs. We offer flexible customization options tailored to meet the unique specifications and branding goals of every client, making us a trusted partner across industries.

At Potluck Tableware, sustainability is a core commitment. Our products are developed in alignment with international environmental standards, reflecting our dedication to responsible manufacturing. With strict quality assurance protocols at every stage of production and a reliable global logistics network, we ensure timely delivery and dependable service across the world.
