Our Story

Hosting parties is all fun and games, until you find the pile of dirty dishes awating your tired body. Those chic china dishes look pretty but result in hours of scrubbing. Isn't there a cheat code to this dilemma? Well that question racked our brains too and we searched far and wide for an alternative. From this mission Potluck Tableware was born.

Combining the elegance of reusable tableware and the ease of paper products we present our range of crockery that will fit any occasion. And no worries, you can say goodbye by to the flimsy, boring plates that always ruin your meal. Our plates are made with paper stable enough to hold any food item- even the runniest of gravies. The unique and beautiful designs are laminated with food safe coating. So stop buying plastic cutlery for you spontaneous picnics and potlucks, and join us instead.
